It's the return of the Daily Climb this week! Those who have played at least one game can now participate in the Daily Climb. Does the leaderboard work? Do the modifiers cause issues? Have ideas for the daily?? Now's your chance to give feedback.

This patch also brings many changes to the Underdocks including 3 new encounters and lots of balancing and reworks while we continue to incorporate art and animations for these critters. There are also new Necrobinder cards and new Events which aren't exclusive to the Underdocks.

Upon winning or losing, there's now a more dramatic Climb Summary screen. You'll be able to speed it up later, but for now- patiently wait while viewing all the rooms you traversed and the amount of new things you discovered at the end of each climb.
