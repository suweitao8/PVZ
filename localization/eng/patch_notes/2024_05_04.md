Hey playtesters, got another patch for ya! And it's a doozy. First of all... THE NECROBINDER REWORK IS HERE!! No more swapping! Necro's HP is now a fraction of what it was but, at the end of combat, she heals completely AND revives Osty if he's dead! Another big change to her kit is she can now sacrifice her own HP to boost Osty in various ways through cards. We're very interested in hearing your thoughts on her new playstyle!! 💀 ✨ 

Other notable changes are that this is the first Godot build with music and SFX (wooo!) and the Regent's Gravity mechanic has started to be phased out (still a WIP!)

Also, one of the bugs we've been tracking is a particular kind of softlock. We've seen it a couple of times but it's particularly tricky to reproduce. Unlike other softlocks, the common symptom of this one:
- you can't hover or /play cards in your hand (even for tooltips).
- you can still technically hit End Turn and the combat progresses (though obviously you are stuck because you can't play cards)

So far we're calling this bug the hand lock (or hand cramp). If you see it, please file it as such with as much info as you can. Happy slaying!
