Got another patch for you all today and, because of PAX, it's another small one 🤏  Most of the content changes here are actually reworks of Tezcatara's and Zlatir's choices, which are in preparation for a new Ancient coming within the next couple weeks!

**CONTENT:**

Ironclad:
- Reworked Stomp card: “Deal 8 damage. hits an additional time if you've played at least 2 attacks this turn.”

Potions & Relics:
- Reworked Amethyst Bangle relic: “Card rewards can now contain colorless cards.”

Ancients:
- Removed Tezcatara’s Kindle options
- Reworked Tezcatara’s Ember choice: now only affects basic Strikes
- Reworked Vlad’s Wealth choice: “Gain 999 gold. All but 1 shop becomes a ? room”
- Reworked Zlatir’s Acknowledgement choice: no longer adds Scorned to cards
- Reworked Zlatir’s Immortal choice: now gives you Cursed Blood (gain 50% less block) instead of 4 Decay
- Reworked Zlatir’s Power choice: gives you 1 Folly (while in hand, take 1 damage whenever you play a card) instead of 3 Normalities

**BALANCE:**

Regent:
- Nerfed Parry card: no longer Forges

Colorless Cards:
- Changed Anointed card: upgrade changed from Innate -> Retain

Enemies:
- Changed Furry Worm Crawler:
 - Damage decreased from 5 -> 4
 - Strength gain increased from 5 -> 7
- Changed Nibbit:
 - Butt damage increased from 13 -> 14
 - Slice damage and Block increased from 6 -> 7
- Buffed Shrinker Beetle: Stomp damage increased from 15 -> 16
- Nerfed The Legend:
 - No longer gains Block or Plating on its Buff turn
 - No longer starts with Plating
 - Weak applied on its Beat Up turn decreased from 2 -> 1
 - Sealed Sword’s Herald of Tempests card's starting damage increased from 1 -> 11

**ART:**

- Added Tezcatara animations
- Added playtester beta art from the Byrdlett server
- Added Molten Fist card VFX
- Added colored card trails for Silent, Defect, Regent, Necrobinder

**USER INTERFACE & EXPERIENCE:**

- Added the keyword Eternal (cannot be removed from your deck)
- Ethereal hovertip now also always comes with Exhaust hovertip
- Overhauled topbar node properties to prevent text shrinkage and overlap
- Resized map drawing buttons a bit
- The dev console can now be opened by the ' key to better support Swedish keyboards

**WORDING:**

- Reworded Zlatir’s Beauty choice
- Updated Stomp card’s wording to make it more clear how much damage you are doing

**BUG FIXES:**

- Photon Blade card now has correct card selection prompt
- Fixed issue preventing you from previewing cards in deck view
- Fixed softlock on power swoosh
- Selecting cards in hand correctly cancels queued card plays
- Strength from a previous combat no longer affects upgrade previews in rest sites
