This week's patch brings a big content and balance pass, especially when it comes to The Necrobinder and The Regent. We're also saying goodbye to our old friend, the "Strength Down" power; whenever you would lose temporary Strength at the end of your turn you will now see a unique power based on the source of the temporary Strength. We're especially interested in getting feedback on how this change feels!
