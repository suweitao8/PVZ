We're back to our regularly scheduled programming this week--another Slay the Spire 2 build is here! In terms of content for this patch we have two new Act 1 bosses and some new Regent cards along with lots of balance and UI/UX updates. And in even bigger news, **we're switching to Steam builds from here on out!**

**IMPORTANT: this means you will have to mark the game as private on Steam so that StS2 and StS2-related activity is hidden on your profile like so:**
**- Activate the game key in Steam**
**- Right-click on Slay the Spire 2 in your library**
**- Click Manage -> Mark as Private**

**We've attached a video demonstrating the process, too. Just DM/ping Casey or Anthony for a key and they'll get you one!**
