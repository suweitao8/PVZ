This week we've scrapped and rebuilt The Defect once again, deprecating and adding many various cards. Also, the Timeline is back! Because of this, you'll find that your progress has been reset and much of the game's content must now be unlocked via the Timeline. We'd especially appreciate any feedback you have on it! Other than that, some events have been rebalanced, art has been added, and bugs have been fixed!
