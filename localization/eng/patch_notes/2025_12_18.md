Happy holidays! This is likely our last major patch of the year (unless we seriously broke stuff)

We have significant changes to the bosses: Doormaker and Knowledge Demon while also bringing in changes and tweaks for various content, notably for events.

You may notice that the game is slightly kinder during the holidays, as several relics have gotten their drop rules updated. Many cards now have updated names as we gear up the content for translation while ancient dialogues get a small clean up. The Timeline's getting some unlock changes soon... in preparation, there's bugfixes, cleanup, and simplification this patch.
