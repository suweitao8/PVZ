Time for another weekly patch! If you make your way on over to the Potion Lab, you'll find it has received a total makeover--and what's more, the potions themselves have received makeovers! Our goal with these new potion assets is to make them more visually distinct from one another and be more representative of what each potion does. Feedback is encouraged!
