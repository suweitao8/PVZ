[blue][b]BALANCE:[/b][/blue]

General:
- Some effects that trigger via block or HP loss "from a card" now trigger "on your turn" instead (Beacon of Hope card, Rupture card, Laughing Stone relic)
- When a power applied by a card gives block (ex. After Image), deals damage (ex. Panache), or causes HP loss (ex. Inferno), the block/damage/HP loss is no longer considered to be "from a card".
- In combination with the new "from a card" -> "on your turn" change in various powers/relics, this should be a buff
- For example, one-off effects triggered by block gain (ex. Unmovable card) will no longer be accidentally consumed by automatic Block gain (ex. After Image card), while permanent effects triggered by Block gain (ex. Beacon of Hope) will be affected by new sources of Block gain (ex. Plating power)
