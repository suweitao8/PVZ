This week, we're still making significant changes to the back end for flexibility and performance improvements so please let us know of any issues like broken textures or egregious hiccups. General feedback about overall performance or quality of certain assets is also appreciated.
Content is still focused on getting the Defect to a good place while making general balance adjustments.

At the start of each run, the player now goes straight to Neow and there's a less intense HP refill effect. This mimics STS1's behavior and saves a click.
The Timeline is functional again and while it still has no affect on gameplay, transitions are smoother, the requirements for obtaining them are no longer random, and some save migration logic has been added so the screen isn't too broken. It's not quite ready for primetime yet.
