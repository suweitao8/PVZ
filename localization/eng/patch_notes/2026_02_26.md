It's the last weekly patch before Slay the Spire 2's Early Access launch!! Thank you to all of our playtesters who have helped get us to this point. Your feedback has been invaluable! These notes are mostly some slight balance adjustments, bug fixes, and visual polish. We're almost to the next stage of StS2's evolution!!

[blue][b]CONTENT:[/b][/blue]

- Achievements have been temporarily disabled for Early Access

[blue][b]BALANCE:[/b][/blue]

[b]Necrobinder:[/b]
- Nerfed [b]Flatten[/b] card: damage decreased from 14(18) -> 12(16)

[b]Potions & Relics:[/b]
- Changed [b]Paper Krane[/b] and [b]Tingsha[/b] relics: rarities swapped
- Changed [b]Razor Tooth[/b] relic: now only upgrades Attacks and Skills

[b]Enemies:[/b]
- Buffed [b]Seapunk[/b]:
  - Sea Kick move damage increased from 9(10) -> 11(12)
  - Strength gain now scales with Ascension from 1(1) -> 1(2)
- Nerfed [b]Test Subject[/b]: Phase 3 Lacerate damage decreased from 11(12) -> 10(11)
- Changed [b]Thieving Hopper[/b]: is now pickier about the cards he steals
  - He will only steal cards that are in your deck (meaning no cards generated in combat)
  - He will try NOT to steal any cards with the Imbued enchantment unless he has no other choices
  - His card rarity priority order is as follows:
    - Priority 1: Uncommon cards
    - Priority 2: Common, Rare, or Event cards
    - Priority 3: Basic or Quest cards
    - Priority 4: Ancient cards or cards with the Imbued enchantment
    - Priority 5: Anything else (in practice, this should only ever be Curses)
