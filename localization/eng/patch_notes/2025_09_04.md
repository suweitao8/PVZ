We're back from PAX + an extended Labor Day holiday, so this week was also a bit of a shorter one. Still, this patch has a bunch of new Ironclad cards, another Knowledge Demon rework, balance changes, and new art!

[blue][b]CONTENT:[/b][/blue]

Ironclad:

- Added new cards
- Cascade
- Hone
- Pyre
- Smolder
- Inferno
- Pillage
- Spite
- Reworked Unmovable card
- Deprecated Combust card

Ancients:

- Deprecated Nonupeipe's Fancy Pin relic/blessing

Enemies:

- Reworked Knowledge Demon

[blue][b]BALANCE:[/b][/blue]

Ironclad:

- Changed Bloodletting card: rarity decreased from Uncommon -> Common
- Changed Stampede card: rarity decreased from Rare -> Uncommon
- Changed Fight Me! card:
- Damage decreased from 20(25) -> 16(20)
- Self Strength gain decreased from 2(3) -> 1(2)
- Enemy Strength gain decreased from 2 -> 1
- Rarity decreased from Rare -> Uncommon

Defect:

- Nerfed Lightning Rod card: turns affected decreased from 2 -> 1

Necrobinder:

- Nerfed Flatten card: damage scaling decreased from 5(8) -> 3(4)
- Buffed Restrain card: upgrade damage increased from +6 -> +8
- Changed Right Hand Hand card:
- Damage increased from 5(7) -> 8(11)
- Cost increased from 0 -> 1

Potions & Relics:

- Nerfed Pulsing Core relic: turn count increased from every 3 turns -> every 4

Enemies:

- Reworked Illusion monsters like Eye With Teeth and Parafright: no longer have infinite HP, and they no longer have both the Illusion and Disrupt power. Instead, when an Illusion is "killed", it immediately revives to full HP and is stunned.
- Buffed Living Shield: Smash move damage increased from 13(15) -> 16(18)
- Buffed Queen: Strength applied to Torchhead increased from 3 -> 4
- Buffed Terror Eel: HP increased from 131(133) -> 140(150)

[blue][b]BUG FIXES:[/b][/blue]

General:

- Each act now properly shows its own different Rest Site visuals again

- Events should no longer go into your won fights in the progress file
- Fixed being unable to open potion popup in shop

Necrobinder:

- If Invoke card is Transfigured and then Osty attacks, Invoke now properly costs 0 instead of 1

Potions & Relics:

Ancients:

Enemies:

Multiplayer:
