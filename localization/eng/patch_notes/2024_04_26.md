Patch time!!! We have a lot more bug fixes as usual, but most notably we have a lot of enemy reworks. For instance, the Burrowing Bug is no longer untargetable when burrowed and the Queen and her Torch Head Amalgam have some fresh new moves.

Also the Regent is looking SO SNAZZY with his new animations, assets, and card art!! Revel in our special boy's glory ✨ 

By the way, some things we have planned changes for are Shattered, Byrdonis, and Gravity, so don't worry about those too much for now. In the meantime, let's dig into this!

**CONTENT**

- Implemented the new Bound card affliction
 - Bound cards cannot be played
 - Bound will be removed if the afflicted card is in your hand at the end of your turn

**GAMEPLAY**
- Effects that damage enemies at the end of your turn now trigger before your hand is discarded, preventing Inevitability from triggering Gremlin Horn after hand is discarded and leaving a card in your hand for next turn
- The Defect's end-of-turn orb effects now trigger before your hand is discarded

**BALANCE:**

Enemies:
- Burrowing Bug completely reworked
 - HP from 91-94 -> 85-91
 - Burrowed reworked, now gains 30 Block on burrowing, block is not removed at the start of the turn while burrowed
 - If all block is removed, the Burrowing Bug will emerge from below
 - Moveset changes to be fixed and non random.
- Hunter Killer reworked
 - HP increased from 110 -> 121
 - Bite Damage increased from 18 -> 20
 - Puncture -> now adds 3 Wounds in addition to damage
 - Tenderizing Goop Vulnerable debuff increased from 2 -> 4
 - Bravery power removed
- Queen completely reworked
 - New moves
   - You're Mine: Applies 5 Weak, Vulnerable, and Frail
   - Puppet Strings: Applies Bound to 4 cards in your draw pile
 - Revised move order
- Torch Head Amalgam reworked
 - HP decreased 400 -> 375
 - New moves
  - Tackle: Deals 7x3 damage
  - Protected: Gives the Queen 35 block
  - Hug: Makes enemy lose 3 Dexterity and Strength this turn
- Orb Plant reworked
 - HP decreased from 36-39 -> 31-36
 - New attacks
  - Charge Energy: Gains 5 strength
  - Fire Energy: Deals 8 damage
  - Revised move order: Charge -> Charge -> Fire
- Chomper now only takes 5 hits instead of 9 to be unclamped and stunned
- It is now disallowed for 2 Tracker Raiders to show up in the same Ruby Raider fight

Cards:
- Ashen Strike rarity changed from Common -> Uncommon
- Quasar changed
 - Damage increased from 15 -> 33(44)
 - Cost no longer decreases on upgrade
- Voidborn Block gain decreased from 4(5) -> 3(4)
