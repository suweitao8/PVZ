All of this unlocking business revealed we needed some more content and so we're bringing in 17 relics and 4 potions. We're also adding some logic that makes it so you encounter each boss until you've seen them all.

There's also first time user experience stuff going in. Players will encounter a different boss each Act until you've fought them all. Afterwards, they will be randomized as usual. This feature exists in the first game as well, don't tell anyone. Similar logic exists for the very first run that you do, you'll always fight our simplest enemy on that run.

Necrobinder and Defect continue to get content and balance passes as we clean up some bugs from a lot of the combat logic cleanups which we're still bringing in.

Finally, there's a big speed up for card play as cards now kick off their effects while the card is flying towards the center upon playing (rather than after the card finishes flying to the center). Thanks to ocean for pointing it out :). Okay, have a nice week.
