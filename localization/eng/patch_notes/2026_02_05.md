Welcome to the first patch of February 2026! This one is more medium-sized, with a few balance and logic changes, a couple of reworks, and some art and UI additions.

[b]CONTENT:[/b]

[b]Silent:[/b]
- Added returning card from StS1, Expertise
- Deprecated Graceful card

[b]Necrobinder:[/b]
- Reworked Countdown card

[b]Regent:[/b]
- Reworked Black Hole card

[b]BALANCE:[/b]

[b]Ironclad:[/b]
- Changed [b]Grapple[/b] card: now applies a debuff to the enemy instead of a buff to the player

[b]Defect:[/b]
- Changed [b]Signal Boost[/b] card: when played twice, it now doubles the next two Powers played, rather than increasing the doubling to tripling

[b]Necrobinder:[/b]
- Osty now uses the player's block when he is dealt damage directly (for example, when using an Osty attack on an enemy with Thorns)
- Buffed [b]Bodyguard[/b] card: Summon increased from 4(6) -> 5(7)
- Nerfed [b]Calcify[/b] card: damage decreased from 5(7) -> 4(6)
- Changed [b]Friendship[/b] card:
  - No longer lowers Dexterity
  - Strength loss increased from 1 -> 2(1)
  - No longer gains Innate on Upgrade
- Buffed [b]Unleash[/b] card: base damage increased from 5(8) -> 6(9)

[b]Potions & Relics:[/b]
- Changed [b]Vambrace[/b] relic: now doubles Block gain from Melancholy card again
- Changed [b]Dragon Fruit[/b] and [b]Planisphere[/b] relics: no longer appear after the Act 3 treasure chest

[b]Enemies:[/b]
- When playing [b]Hellraiser[/b] card against the [b]Queen[/b] boss, Strikes are now auto-played before Chains of Binding power afflicts them with Bound, meaning they no longer count as the Bound card played this turn
- Nerfed [b]Knowledge Demon[/b]: Ponder move Strength gain decreased from 3(4) -> 2(3)
- Nerfed [b]Magi Knight[/b]: Bomb move damage decreased from 40(45) -> 35(40)
- Buffed [b]Spectral Knight[/b]:
  - Soul Slash move damage increased from 13(15) -> 15(17)
  - Now always does Soul Slash after Hex move

[b]Ancients:[/b]
- Nerfed [b]Neow's Fury[/b] card: no longer heals HP
- Changed Vakuu's [b]Potential[/b] blessing:
  - Can no longer give 2 of the same curse
  - Can no longer give Greed curse

[b]Events:[/b]
- Changed [b]Hungry for Mushrooms[/b] event: relics now modify your HP by static amounts instead of percentage-based amounts (Big Mushroom gives you 20 max HP instead of 25% and Fragrant Mushroom makes you lose 15 HP instead of 20%)
