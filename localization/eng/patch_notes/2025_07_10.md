This week's patch graces you with the presence of a new Act 3 Ancient, whose name may seem oddly familiar... Also, the Test Subject boss has received a major rework, maps have been slightly rebalanced, various UI/UX improvements have been made, and lots of new card and beta art has been added!

[blue][b]CONTENT:[/b][/blue]

Ancients:
- Added new Act 3 Ancient, Nonupeipe

Potions & Relics:
- Reworked Merchant's Robes relic into a new Nonupeipe relic

Enemies:
- Reworked Test Subject

[blue][b]BALANCE:[/b][/blue]

Defect:
- Nerfed Glass Orb: starting value decreased by 1
- Changed Adaptive Strike card: rarity increased from Uncommon -> Rare
- Changed Genetic Algorithm card: rarity increased from Uncommon -> Rare
- Buffed Hotfix card: Focus gain increased from 1(2) -> 2(3)
- Nerfed Refract card: damage decreased from 11(13) -> 9(11)

Regent:
- Buffed Conqueror card: upgraded damage increased from +1 -> +4

Enemies:
- Nerfed Fabricator: big attack damage decreased from 25(28) -> 18(21)
- Nerfed Infested Guardian: no longer attacks when it does its Adapt move
- Nerfed Magi Knight:
- Block gain on first Shield decreased from 8 -> 5
- Shield damage decreased from 8(9) -> 6(7)
- Spear damage decreased from 15(17) -> 10(11)

Events:
- Changed Sunken Treasury event: no longer has a medium chest option
- Changed Trash Heap event: no longer gives the player Gold

General:
- Increased Shop count to 3 and decreased Elite count to 5 per map
- The Draft modifier now presents rewards in order and no longer lets you skip

[blue][b]USER INTERFACE & EXPERIENCE:[/b][/blue]

- Added hovertip for Whistle card's Stun effect
- After Image power and Barnacled Pipe relic now grant block much faster
- Boss nodes are no longer hoverable if you cannot travel to it
- Changing the boss in the Reflections event now updates the top bar UX
- Got rid of the room icon at the top of the Run History screen
- Poison damage ticks now have the same wait time regardless of whether they kill enemies
- Reduced screenshake and audio volume when you skip through the game over animation
- Trash Heap event cards are now considered event cards in the compendium
- When Necro dies to Doom, Osty dies as well

[blue][b]WORDING:[/b][/blue]

- Renamed Enfeeble card to Debilitate in order to avoid confusion with Enfeebling Touch card
- Improved wording for Bombardment and Cinder cards
- Fixed capitalization error in the Channel hovertip
- Improved Smoggy affliction's wording and tooltip
- Updated wording for static Glass Orb description on cards

[blue][b]BUG FIXES:[/b][/blue]

Ironclad:
- Eternal Armor card and Oddly Heavy Stone relic now combine to properly give twice as much block instead of four times as much block
- Eternal Armor card now properly consumes the trigger of relics like Vambrace and Oddly Heavy Stone

Silent:
- Fixed an issue where killing an enemy with Poison and Accelerant could cause a softlock

Defect:
- All For One card now properly retrieves cards from the discard pile if their cost has been reduced to 0 via a passive effect like Feral card
- Refract card now deals damage before applying the new Orbs

Necrobinder:
- Bound Phylactery now properly triggers after Friendship card, preventing it from permanently giving you 1 extra energy
- Mischief card no longer triggers if unblocked damage is 0
- Fixed various visual issues with Osty and Sandpit
- Hand Drill relic is now properly triggered when Osty breaks an enemy's block
- Osty now actually shakes when you play an Osty attack while it's dead
- Sleight of Flesh card now properly triggers when applying negative amounts of certain buffs (like Strength)
- Spirit of Ash card no longer incorrectly triggers itself when it's been given Ethereal by an effect like Spectral Knight monster's Hex power

Regent:
- Regent no longer has a Neow bundle with a rare card; Beat Into Shape card replaced by Wrought in War card
- Black Hole card now decreases Star cost on upgrade
- Fixed an issue where, if you had Oracle Bone relic and you played Void Form as your first card, your second turn would end immediately after it started
- Glow card no longer exhausts
- Heavenly Drill card no longer hits all enemies
- Pillar of Creation card now properly triggers when a card is transformed

Colorless Cards:
- Fixed an interaction between Mayhem and Relax
- When Mayhem would auto-play Relax, its extra card draw would trigger immediately, while its energy gain would wait until next turn. Now, both properly wait until next turn.
- Fixed delay after Automation power would trigger
- The second hit from Omnislice card now properly triggers Phantasmal Gardener monster's Skittish power

Enemies:
- Knowledge Demon's intent no longer overlaps with the relic bar

Ancients:
- Folly curse card can no longer be upgraded
- Tanx transforming cards into Maul preview no longer shows broken card descriptions

General:
- Cards in queue now properly disappear after combat ends
- Echoing enchantment no longer duplicates the Echo keyword
- Fixed a number of issues caused by temporary card energy cost offsets clearing static temporary card energy costs before applying
- Fixed an interaction between on-shuffle effects and card draw
- Fixed sorting issue in deck view between cards of different colors
- Scrolling through climb histories should now be less laggy

Multiplayer:
- Fixed an issue with Phantasmal Gardener monster's Skittish power in multiplayer
- Gravity Well card's power no longer triggers when a teammate plays a card
- Bound affliction no longer counts other players playing bound cards as your own in multiplayer
- Fixed softlock when playing Sly Acrobatics from Tools of the Trade power
- Fixed stuck cards when playing them before Tools of the Trade power triggers
- You can no longer hover your own player intent anymore
- Osty is no longer summoned by dead players' Bound Phylacteries
